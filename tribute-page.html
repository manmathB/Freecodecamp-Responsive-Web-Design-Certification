Paul Scholes

A midfield maestro

Here are few of the highlights of Scholes' career

  • Reached FA youth cup final in 1993
  • Made his debut vs Port Vale in 2-1 victory where he scored both goals
  • Won premier league 11 times
  • Won FA cup three times
  • He was also part of treble winning side of Manchester United in 1998-99 season
  • Retired and then came back from retirement and won against noisy neighbours
  • Scored a goal in 19 consecutive premier league seasons
